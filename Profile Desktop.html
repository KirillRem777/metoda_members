About Me

I am a seasoned strategic consultant with over 15 years of experience helping Fortune 500 companies navigate complex business transformations. My passion lies in bridging the gap between innovative technology solutions and practical business applications.

Throughout my career, I have led numerous digital transformation initiatives, focusing on organizational change management and strategic planning. I believe in the power of collaborative leadership and have mentored over 50 professionals in their career development journey.

When I'm not consulting, I enjoy contributing to industry research, speaking at conferences, and exploring new technologies that can drive business innovation. I hold an MBA from Harvard Business School and a Ph.D. in Organizational Psychology from Stanford University.
